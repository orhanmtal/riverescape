Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to River Escape Elite. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game.

2. Data Collection

Our game is designed to provide a premium experience with minimal data collection. We may collect non-personal data such as:

  • High scores and game progress (stored via Google Play Games Services).
  • Device information (model, OS version) for optimization.
  • In-game currency (gold) balance.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own policies:

  • Google Play Services: For leaderboards and achievements.
  • AdMob: To display ads. We comply with Google's Families Policy and COPPA/GDPR-K for child-directed content.
  • Firebase: For cloud synchronization of game states and global leaderboards.

4. Children's Privacy

River Escape Elite is family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our ad configurations are set to "G" rated content to ensure a safe environment for all ages.
